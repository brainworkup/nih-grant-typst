Key Phases:
-----------
1. SETUP: Template selection and configuration
2. DEVELOP: Core document creation and editing
3. REVIEW: Content validation and quality checks
4. REVISE: Iterative improvements
5. SUBMIT: Final preparation and delivery

Quality Gates:
--------------
✓ Page limits (R01: 12pg, R03/R21: 6pg)
✓ Required sections complete
✓ References formatted
✓ Figures/tables labeled
✓ Budget justified
✓ Biosketches current

Critical Decision Points:
------------------------
• Grant Type: Determines page limits and scope
• Content Review: Scientific merit assessment
• Quality Check: Format and completeness
• Feedback: Major vs minor revision path
